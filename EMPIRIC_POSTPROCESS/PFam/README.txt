- IGPS family alignment obtained from PFam (PF00218).

- Suffix “fixed” here means, that lower case amino acid letters were replaced with upper case, - nothing else.

- HMM matrix seed obtained from PFam, and stored here just in case.

- annotation stores a map between PFam sequence ids and SS,Tm,Tt organisms of interest.

- KZ thermpfam stores a map between organisms in PFam MSA, their readable name and T_env - optimal growth or environmental temperature. Other scripts were used as well, to perform this map, but they are deprecated by now.
